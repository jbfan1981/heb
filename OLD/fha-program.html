FHA Program

FHA Program Offers Personalized Assistance. Each H-E-B family will have access to a Family Health Assistant (FHA) to help you manage your health care needs. Available Monday – Friday, from 8 a.m. to 5:30 p.m., your FHA will

  • Answer questions and provide local resources
  • Direct you to local preferred providers
  • Help you coordinate your care
  • Assist with claims review
  • Contact a provider’s office if you have questions
  • Collaborate with providers and help you to get second opinions

Additionally, there is an H-E-B Crisis intervention team available 24/7.

Condition Management

If you have a chronic condition or are at risk for one, this program offers you education, coaching and help in monitoring your condition. The Blue Care® Advisors, made up of registered nurses or other health care professionals, will help you

  • Identify unhealthy behaviors,
  • Set wellness goals,
  • Adopt healthier habits and
  • Learn to manage medical conditions more effectively.
